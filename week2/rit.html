About Ramaiah Institute of Technology

Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT), established in 1962, is one of the premier institutes in Bangalore, known for its academic excellence and state-of-the-art infrastructure. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in engineering and technology.

Courses Offered

Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech)

Specializations: Computer Science, Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Electronics, and more.

Master of Technology (M.Tech)

Specializations: Computer Science, Structural Engineering, VLSI Design, and more.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Specializations: Marketing, HR, Finance, and Operations.

Admissions

Admissions to RIT are based on the Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) for undergraduate courses and GATE/PGCET for postgraduate courses. For detailed information about eligibility and the admission process, please visit our official Admissions Page.
